NaukriPlus Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Data Collection

NaukriPlus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing data, or any other user data. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not communicate with any external servers.

How It Works

The extension reads API responses from www.naukri.com's own job-listing endpoint to display accurate applicant counts on job listing pages. All processing happens locally on your device. No data leaves your browser.
